About

This is a bridge to swap sats bewteen L1 and L2 in the Lightning Network.
I put below some questions you may have.

What are the limits?
The limits are pretty low, from min 1000 sats to just about max 10000 sats, this is just an experiment right now. I'm working to make it better.

Do you have a Lightning Node?
Yes, wanna connect to it? Check it out!

Is this swap custodial?
Right now, Yes. Future ideas include non-custodial ways using eltoo contracts and pools to gain interest in your lightning channels.

What tools or framework do you use?
Everything in this site is plain HTML+CSS+JS, no frameworks, neither in frontend or backend. The backend is just a shell script talking to LND.
